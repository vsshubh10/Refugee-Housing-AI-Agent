SHELTER MIND

Shelter Mind is a humanitarian tool that helps relief teams quickly generate practical and culturally sensitive shelter blueprints based on local materials, climate, and budget constraints.

Principles

  • Safety first - advice on ventilation, thermal comfort, and structural stability.
  • Local materials & skills - designs that match what's available on the ground.
  • Cultural sensitivity - layouts that respect privacy, gender separations, and local practices.
